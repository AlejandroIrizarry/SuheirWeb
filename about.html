Get to know Suheir Irizarry Designs

Suheir Irizarry Negrón

Interior Designer & 3D Artist

Suheir Irizarry Designs is a new small business founded by Suheir Irizarry Negrón on 2021. Suheir is a recent graduate from the Polythectic University of Puerto Rico with a Bachelors Degree on Architecture Interior Designs. Suheir Irizarry Designs mission is to bring to life out of the spectrum designs by transforming simple spaces and ideas into stunning designs.

Suheir attributes her keen eye for tropical and minimalistic designs that allow an interior feel like a heavenly space while also provoking a peerless feeling of amusement on her client. Most of her designs are distinguishable for using elements focused on biomimicry and ecomarine life. Through her designs, she hopes to inspire other to develop ecoawareness and to spark attention about important topics like sustainability.

Suheir specializes on in-home and online interior design consultations & project developments. She is also passionate about 3D rendering, where she has been able to create fun-looking animations and compositions. Check it out!
